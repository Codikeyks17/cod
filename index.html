De La Salle University Spikers

I wanted to join the volleyball club because I have a strong interest in sports and teamwork. Volleyball is not just a game for me—it’s an opportunity to grow physically, mentally, and socially. I believe being part of a volleyball team will help me improve my fitness, coordination, and discipline. It also gives me the chance to work with others, communicate better, and build strong friendships through shared goals and experiences. Joining the volleyball club allows me to challenge myself and step out of my comfort zone. I want to develop my skills, learn proper techniques, and eventually contribute to the team’s success. More than winning, I value the lessons learned through hard work, dedication, and persistence. I know that through practices and matches, I will become not only a better athlete but also a more responsible and confident person. Overall, I see the volleyball club as a great way to combine my passion with personal development. It motivates me to stay active, support my teammates, and strive for excellence on and off the court.





Codi Joseph G. Castañeda
Grade 11 - Production
