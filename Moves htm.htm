breakdown of the common positions:
Setter (S): The setter orchestrates the offense, making the second contact to set up the hitters.

Outside Hitter (OH): Also known as the left-side hitter, they are a primary attacker and play in the front and back rows.

Opposite Hitter (Opp or RS): Plays opposite the setter and is another key attacker.

Middle Blocker (MB or MH): Plays near the net, blocking opposing hitters and also hitting quick attacks.

Libero (L): A defensive specialist who can freely substitute in and out, focusing on passing and digging.

Volleyball is a team sport where two teams of six players each try to score points by sending a ball over a net and landing it within the opponent's court.
